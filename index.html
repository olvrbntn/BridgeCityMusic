Bridge City Music

We sell vintage guitars, basses, synths, and more.

We are an independent guitar shop located in the heart of Southeast Portland. Our technicians are friendly, knowledgeable, and enthusiastic to help you find that next special piece of gear you've been looking for! We're open from 8am-6pm Monday-Friday and also offer co-sign sales, trades, and repair services.

Repair Services
